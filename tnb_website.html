Who We Are

About The New Breed

A grassroots movement turning potential into power — one young Nigerian at a time.

Our Story

Building Futures Since 2014

The New Breed for Sustainable Development (TNB) was founded to address the growing challenge of youth unemployment and skills gaps in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. As a grassroots NGO, we go directly to communities — identifying talented young people and giving them the practical skills and confidence to become self-reliant.

Since our founding, we have trained over 4,700 young people across vocational, digital, and civic programs. Our ambitious target is to reach 10,000 youths across all 31 local government areas of Akwa Ibom State.

We operate primarily from our headquarters in Uyo, with active chapters in Ukanafun and expanding into more LGAs every year.

Engr. Ekpenyong Udo Ekpenyong

Founder & Executive Director

A visionary engineer and community leader who established TNB with a deep belief that empowered youth are the cornerstone of sustainable development. Under his leadership, TNB has grown from a local initiative into a recognized force for change in Akwa Ibom State, directly impacting thousands of young lives through skills training, entrepreneurship support, and civic mobilization.

What We Teach

Training Programs

Comprehensive, hands-on vocational training designed to equip youth with marketable skills and the entrepreneurial mindset to thrive.

Our Departments

Courses Offered

Technology

ICT & Digital Skills Training

Our ICT program equips participants with foundational and intermediate computer skills, preparing them for the digital economy. Trainees gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and software in a fully equipped computer lab.

  • Computer Fundamentals & MS Office Suite
  • Internet Usage & Digital Communication
  • Data Entry & Administrative Computing
  • Basic Web Browsing & Online Business
  • Digital Literacy for Entrepreneurs
Vocational

Fashion Design & Tailoring

Our fashion department trains participants in garment construction, pattern making, and the business of fashion. Students use professional sewing machines and learn techniques applicable to both local and international markets.

  • Machine & Hand Sewing Techniques
  • Pattern Making & Fabric Cutting
  • Traditional & Contemporary Garment Construction
  • Fashion Business & Client Management
  • Finishing & Quality Control
Hospitality

Catering & Food Production

Our catering program transforms passion for food into a professional skill set. Participants learn food preparation, presentation, safety, and how to run a successful catering business — from small events to large-scale operations.

  • Professional Food Preparation & Cooking
  • Baking, Pastry & Confectionery
  • Food Hygiene & Safety Standards
  • Event Catering & Service Management
  • Food Business & Cost Management
Beauty

Makeover & Beauty Arts

Our makeover department trains participants in professional makeup artistry, skincare, and the business of beauty. Graduates are equipped with tools — including a professional makeup kit — and the skills to launch their own beauty studios.

  • Professional Makeup Artistry
  • Skincare & Facial Treatments
  • Bridal & Event Makeup
  • Hair Styling Basics
  • Beauty Business Setup & Client Acquisition
Crafts

Leather Works & Craftsmanship

This program covers the art and craft of leatherwork — from basic techniques to advanced production of shoes, bags, belts, and accessories. Top performers receive professional starter equipment to launch their workshops.

  • Leather Cutting & Shaping Techniques
  • Shoe Making & Cobbling
  • Bag & Accessory Production
  • Equipment Operation & Maintenance
  • Leather Business & Market Strategies

Celebrating Achievement

Graduation & Starter Packs

Completing a TNB program is a milestone — graduates are celebrated at a formal ceremony and outstanding performers receive professional starter equipment to launch their businesses.

More Than a Certificate

At TNB, graduation is just the beginning. We equip our best graduates with professional tools — from sewing machines to bench grinders, makeup kits to kitchen equipment — so they can immediately start their entrepreneurial journey.

Our graduation ceremonies are attended by government officials, community leaders, and families — recognizing the hard work of every trainee and celebrating their transformation from students to entrepreneurs.

Top performers across all departments are identified and rewarded, ensuring that excellence is recognized and the transition into business ownership is supported.

🎓 Entrepreneurship Support: Based on performance during training, graduates receive professional starter packs valued to help them immediately launch and sustain their businesses.
